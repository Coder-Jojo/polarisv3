Regarding Fee Payment

No cash payment option will be available at the institute on 23rd July. As enquired for payment mode, following options are made available:


  • Option 1 ‐ SBI I-Collect Net banking facility. Click Here!
  • Option 2 ‐ Offline mode to pay in SBI (any branch):
    A student can exercise SBI Branch option published in SBI Collect payment options page (at lower left corner) and can go to any SBI branch for paying the fee amount either in cash or otherwise acceptable by the bank.
  • Option 3 ‐ Banking mode other than SBI Collect:
    Institute accepts Demand Draft/Bankers Cheque favouring "Director IIT Bhilai" payable at Raipur (Chhattisgarh).

Note: Direct fund transfer through NEFT/RTGS/Bank transfer mode is discouraged due to reconciliation issue and accordingly institute bank details are not shared.
